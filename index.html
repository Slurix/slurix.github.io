About Me

I'm a Chemical Engineer with a B.Sc. from the University of Saskatchewan, specializing in advanced process control systems for the pulp and paper industry.

My expertise spans multiple critical process areas including bleach plant optimization, oxygen delignification systems, recovery boilers, and lime kilns. I've successfully implemented advanced process control solutions across these operations, delivering measurable improvements in efficiency, quality, and operational stability.

Beyond traditional APC implementation, I lead the development of innovative software solutions for control loop monitoring and tuning. This includes building Mode tracking software to monitor controller operational states, identify when loops are in manual vs automatic, and flag automation issues. I also develop custom web reports and APIs to deliver real-time performance insights to operations teams.
